Overview on HR Analytics By-Akanksha Goel

After acquiring knowledge from different sources about analytics,It can be thought as dealing with customer facing problems.Analytics is usually considered by analyzing Big data provided by customers.But there are some internal operations of an organiation such as Human Resource Management that can be benfitted hugely by combining techniques of Analytics in it.

Human Resources when converted to Data-Driven Human resources enable organizations to use collected employee data for better understanding of there workforce and improve overall performance of the company.

Human Resource analytics (HR analytics) is the systematic identification and quantification of the people drivers of business outcomes (Heuvel & Bondarouk, 2016).HR analytics is about analyzing an organization's people problems.

Let us see how Hr Analytics help in development of an organization.

Objectives of HR Analytics

Hr Analytics deals with-

  • Making better decisions for increasing employee satisfaction and retention rates.
  • Knowing about the best employees that are asset to the organization that would help taking actions to retain them for better revenue of the company.Identification of high value employees at the risk of leaving.
  • Examining which factors were correlated with success in the job enabling the company to hire productive workforce therefore improving recruitment process.
  • Assessing the key performance Indicators(KPIs) for measurement of performance Management.
  • Assessment of Recruitment channels that deal with identifying from where the major workForce comes from.
  • Identification of Employees who require further training for the betterment of the organization.
  • Testing the effectiveness of HR policies and different interventions.

Questions dealt with Analytics

  • What are the factors affecting employee satisfaction and productivity?
  • Who are the employees that are asset to the organization?
  • What are the attributes of productive employees?and How attributes are correlated?For Example How working hours are related to productivity of Employees.
  • Which employee require effective training?
  • What is the employee turnover of different branches of the organization?What are the factors affecting turnover of the organization?What is regrettable churn?

Data Acquisition

After acquiring the questions,the nest step is to get Data.Data can be acquired from organization system comprising employee data, pay information, engagement scores etc. Things like performance reviews and email content can contain interesting information for analysis.

Data from email activity is constantly being updated, so any changes in working patterns will show up very quickly. Because they show actual activity, these data are likely to be more accurate than opinion surveys, and offer a genuine way to identify and resolve a major problem.

Social Media data is rapidly changing and updated source of information about employees and hiring managers. Social platform such as glassdoor.com can provide reviews of employees that can be analysed for understanding the employee satisfaction.

Aspects of HR Analytics

Given by Bernard Marr different Aspects of Analytics that must be understood to solve people's problem:-

Competency acquisition analytics

The success of your business depends on the level of expertise and skill of the workforce.Capability Analytics identifies the core competencies that is required by the company and therefore finding gaps in current level of capabilities within the business.

Capacity analytics

Capacity analytics deals with how operationally efficient people are in a business.It also allows businesses to understand how much capacity they have to grow?

Employee churn analytics

Employee churn analytics is the process of assessing your staff turnover rates in an attempt to predict the future and reduce employee churn. Historical employee churn can be identified through traditional KPIs such as the employee satisfaction index, employee engagement level and staff advocacy score.Some employee churn can be desirable.It is important to develop system to stop the regrettable churn.

Corporate culture Analytics

Corporate culture analytics is the process of understanding more about corporate culture or the different cultures that exists across your organization.This ensure recruitment of people that don’t clash with the corporate culture.

Recruitment channel analytics

Recruitment channel analytics is the process of working out where best employees come from and what recruitment channels are most effective.

Employee performance analytics

Employee performance analytics seeks to assess individual employee performance. The resulting insights can identify who is performing well and who may need some additional training or support in order to raise their game.

Predictive Analytics for HR Analytics

Predictive analytics involve a set of various statistical (data mining) techniques used to predict uncertain outcomes. HR possesses a massive amount of people data. By applying predictive analysis to this data, HR relies on data-driven predictive models, instead of relying on gut feeling and soft science. HR predictive analytics enable HR to forecast the impact of people policies on the well-being, happiness and bottom line performance of employees.

Application of Predictive Analytics in HR Management

  • Company estimates the probability of people leaving the company by applying HR predictive analysis.
  • Using Predictive Analytics,it is possible to predict someone’s personality and future work performance based on their Facebook profile.

Natural Language Processing in HR Analytics

Natural Language Processing can be used to analyze performance reviews to create employee competency profiles or automatically generate performance scores for both employees and managers.

Sentiment analysis in email traffic.NLP can be used to predict employee’s engagement and attitudes in different groups by scanning email data.

Resume classification and scoring using NLP can be of great help at the time of recruitment process. Surveys and feedback analysis is another application of NLP in HR perspective.

Business Intelligence Tools for HR Analytics

Business intelligence (BI) tools help in giving inferences and correlations between different attributes in HR Data. BI tools are used for- Aggregating Data Visualizing Data Analyze Data Report Data

Some of the basic BI tools in HR are Qlik, Visier, Tableau, and Power BI.
